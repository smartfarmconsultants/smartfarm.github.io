Rooted in Kenya,
Growing with You

SmartFarm Consultants Kenya, founded and led by Thomas Muthama, is a premier agricultural consultancy empowering farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses across Kenya with the knowledge, tools, and strategy to succeed in modern markets.

We bridge the gap between traditional farming knowledge and modern agricultural science — delivering practical, profitable, and sustainable outcomes across Machakos, Nairobi, and the wider region.

👑 Founder & CEO

Thomas Muthama

Lead Agricultural Consultant · Machakos, Kenya

Thomas Muthama is a seasoned agricultural consultant with over a decade of hands-on experience in smart farming, agribusiness development, and rural livelihood improvement across Kenya. He bridges the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern agronomy — equipping smallholder farmers with the tools, knowledge, and market connections they need to thrive in today's economy.

Under his leadership, SmartFarm Consultants Kenya has transformed 500+ farms across 10+ counties, delivering measurable yield improvements and sustainable income growth for farming families.
